HOW ESCAPE IN 60 IS DIFFERENT FROM OTHER ESCAPE ROOMS



Locally Owned and Operated

We pride ourselves on being a locally operated family business in Charleston, SC. All of our games and puzzles are created in-house and we hire local talent to help make our vision for each room a reality.

The Family Touch

Escape in 60 is owned and operated by a local family whose passion for creating the best possible experience is visible in every part of our guest experience.

Convenient Location

Escape in 60 is located in the heart of Charelston on the historic Market Street. This makes it easy to incorporate our games into a full day of downtown activity.

Fun for Everyone

Escape in 60 offers an experience that will suit every guest. Families with children will love escaping a sinking pirate ship in Davy Jones' Locker or exploring an ancient tomb in The Egyptian Chamber. Want something more intense? No problem! The Ransom room offers a spine-tingling experience like no other. Wishing for a fun vacation? Let us transport you to Vegas in Roulette Me Out. Still not sure which room to book? Give us a call and we'll find the perfect experience for you and your group.

Group Experiences

We cater to all types of group events. We have experience hosting bachelor/bachlorette parties, birthday parties, team-building events, corporate events, youth/church groups and more! Check out our GROUPS page to learn more!
